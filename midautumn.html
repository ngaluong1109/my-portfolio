Midautumn Festival

1. Live: By celebrating diversity and fostering understanding among students from different cultural backgrounds.
2. Learn: Through cultural exchange, language learning, and developing critical thinking skills.
3. Lead: By organizing the event, students can gain leadership experience and enhance their teamwork and problem-solving abilities.
In summary, the event promotes a multicultural environment, facilitates learning, and cultivates leadership skills among participants in the university.
