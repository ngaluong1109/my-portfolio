Resident Assistant for DKU Residence life

As a Resident Assistant, I create various events and contribute to the fun and exciting student life in DKU.

Event Discription

1. After indulging in a week of dedicated studying, it's time to unwind and connect. Join us this Friday night for an experience that perfectly blends excitement and relaxation. We're setting the stage for an unforgettable student gathering where delectable snacks are just the beginning. Picture yourself in the cozy company of friends, all equally intrigued by the enigmatic storyline of "The Wailing." As the plot unfolds, you'll find yourself drawn into a world of mystery and suspense, where every twist and turn keeps you on the edge of your seat.


2. This isn't just about fulfilling our grocery lists; it's about embracing the joy of community, health, and the great outdoors! Imagine biking through quaint neighborhoods, pedaling alongside friends old and new, enjoying the camaraderie that comes with a shared experience. Feel the rush of endorphins as we power our way to the store, immersing ourselves in the vibrant energy of our community. Let's take this opportunity to connect, connect with one another, and connect with the natural world around us.


3. The event will offer an opportunity for participants of all ages to showcase their creativity by painting tree pots. We will provide plain clay pots, paint brushes, acrylic paints, and other necessary art supplies for participants to use during the event. To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience, we will set up designated painting stations to prevent any accidental paint spills on clothing. To add an element of friendly competition, we propose a "Most Beautiful Painting" contest. At the end of the event, a panel of judges, comprising members of our organization and art enthusiasts from the community, will evaluate the painted tree pots and select the participant with the most beautiful painting. The winner will be awarded a special gift as recognition of their artistic talent and contribution to the event.
